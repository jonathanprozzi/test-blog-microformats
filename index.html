I'm a learner, teacher, technologist, musician, and design enthusiast. I'm passionate about empowering others through technology and creativity. I love discovering new mediums for expression, particularly at the intersection of art and technology. I strive to constantly learn new things and share knowledge with others. I'm the Director of Learning Design at the Digital Harbor Foundation where I develop resources for teaching technology and maker skills. I've worked with youth for nearly 10 years through academic coaching, cognitive training, teaching audio engineering, and as a maker educator. I'm the co-host of Homebrew Website Club Baltimore, a bi-monthly meetup where we work, share, and discuss web projects. I'm passionate about web development, programming, design, and helping others discover pathways for creative expression through technology.
